# Software Ideas Based on Eurasia Group Top Risks 2026

## Source Material
- **Document:** Eurasia Group Top Risks 2026 Report
- **Authors:** Ian Bremmer (President), Cliff Kupchan (Chairman)
- **File:** pardot_download.pdf

## The 10 Top Risks
1. US political revolution
2. Overpowered (China's electrotech dominance vs US)
3. The Donroe Doctrine (US asserting power in Western Hemisphere)
4. Europe under siege (political fragmentation in UK, France, Germany)
5. Russia's second front (hybrid warfare against NATO)
6. State capitalism with American characteristics
7. China's deflation trap
8. AI eats its users (extractive AI business models)
9. Zombie USMCA (North American trade limbo)
10. The water weapon (water as geopolitical leverage)

## Red Herrings (Overstated Risks)
- "Tariff Man" at large (peak tariff disruption already passed)
- Deglobalization (trade is restructuring, not disappearing)
- Spheres of influence (world too interconnected to carve up)
- Sell America (US remains most investable major economy)

## Software Ideas

### 1. Risk Dashboard
**Type:** Web Application
**Description:** Interactive visualization tracking the 10 risks over time with live news feeds, sentiment indicators, and trend analysis.
**Features:**
- Real-time news aggregation categorized by risk
- Risk severity meters updated based on events
- Historical trend charts
- Alert system for escalating risks

### 2. Governance Tracker
**Type:** Web Application
**Description:** Inspired by Eurasia's chart plotting government actions on two axes: "erosion of norms" (y-axis) vs "erosion of checks" (x-axis).
**Features:**
- Plot and track executive actions
- Color-code by outcome (blocked, succeeded, contested)
- Compare across administrations or countries
- Crowdsourced or curated data entry

### 3. Scenario Simulator
**Type:** Interactive Tool
**Description:** "What-if" planning tool letting users explore how risks interconnect and cascade.
**Features:**
- Adjust risk parameters and see ripple effects
- Model business/investment exposure
- Generate probability-weighted scenarios
- Export reports for strategic planning

### 4. Business Exposure Assessment Tool
**Type:** B2B SaaS
**Description:** Companies input their profile (geography, sector, supply chains, trading partners) and receive a customized risk assessment.
**Features:**
- Risk scoring by category
- Supply chain vulnerability mapping
- Recommended hedging strategies
- Peer comparison benchmarks

### 5. Interactive Geopolitical Map
**Type:** Web Application
**Description:** Visual exploration of risks by region with drill-down analysis.
**Features:**
- Heat maps showing risk intensity
- Click regions for detailed breakdowns
- Timeline slider to see evolution
- Layer different risk types

**Type:** Game / Educational App
**Description:** Learn geopolitics through interactive scenarios, quizzes, and decision-making simulations.
**Features:**
- Quiz modes testing knowledge of risks
- Role-play scenarios (play as a world leader, CEO, investor)
- Branching narratives based on choices
- Leaderboards and achievements
- Bite-sized learning modules

### 7. Risk Correlation Engine
**Type:** Analytical Tool
**Description:** Shows how the 10 risks interconnect and influence each other.
**Features:**
- Network graph visualization
- Correlation matrices
- Causal chain analysis
- "If X happens, then Y" predictions

### 8. AI-Powered Risk Briefing Generator
**Type:** AI Tool
**Description:** Generates personalized daily/weekly risk briefings based on user interests and portfolio.
**Features:**
- Natural language summaries
- Customizable focus areas
- Integration with calendar/email
- Voice briefing option

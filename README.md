# Crisis Room Brasil 2026

A bilingual (Portuguese/English) geopolitical decision simulator based on the [Eurasia Group Top Risks 2026](https://www.eurasiagroup.net/issues/top-risks-2026) report.

## Play the Game

Open `game_brasil.html` in any modern web browser - no server required.

## Features

- **5 Brazil-centric scenarios** exploring geopolitical risks from a Brazilian perspective
- **Bilingual support** - toggle between Portuguese and English
- **Branching narratives** - your choices lead to different outcomes
- **Expert insights** - learn from the Eurasia Group report analysis
- **Score tracking** - see how your strategic decisions measure up

## Scenarios

1. **O Ultimato de Trump / The Trump Ultimatum** - Navigate US pressure for critical minerals access
2. **O Dilema da China / The China Dilemma** - Balance Brazil's largest trading partner against US pressure
3. **Venezuela ao Lado / Venezuela Next Door** - Respond to regional crisis on Brazil's border

...And more!

## Based On

Eurasia Group Top Risks 2026 by Ian Bremmer and Cliff Kupchan.
